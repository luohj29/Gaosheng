## Design Concept
![Whale-Design-Chair-(2)](/images/G1830/Whale-Design-Chair-21_1.jpg)

1. The chair design element is the whale's tail, which is active, cute and imaginative. The round design of the seat stands for inclusive, broad-minded. 
2. The Y shape backrest support represents achievement, victory, and challenge.
3. At the same time, it also represents the corporate philosophy of GAOSHENG: free, aspiring, courageous, and unique. 
Whale chair means “Swimming in the ocean of knowledge.”


## Materiel
The most prominent feature of the chair is the back design. -Whale design.which is very strong with two Plastic,inside is hard PP plastic,outside is pliable PA material.The back design is sleek , but it can provides firm support to the back while sitting down. This will help the user feel more secure and comfortable. 

The Whale chair provide more ergonomic support for a variety of seat postures from back-and-forth movement , sideways body swaying and twisting.

![Whale-Design-Chair-(3)](/images/G1830/Whale-Design-Chair-31_1.jpg)

**Material Feature**: Non-toxic, tasteless, impact-resistant, aging resistant, good antifouling performance, delicate plastic lines.

## specification
1. chair body: PP material ( color: red, grey white. blue, green could be choosed)
2. seat support:  use Q235 3mm thickness iron plate.
3. seat cover: PP material ,grey white color. 
4. back support: PA material, grey white color. 
5. armrest: pp material. grey white color.
6. frame: high quality metal tube(∮25*T2.0) with ∮20*T2.0 metel tube inside. 
7. bookshelf: pp material
8. caster: black nylon caster. ∮50mm
9. tablet: face for the black ABS material with black PA nylon material under.  3mm thickness plate inside for strengthening support. hidden nylon cup holder. 
10. The support for the tablet is high quality steel tube (∮22*T2.5 ). can support the weight of 25KG.
## Different Position
![Whale Design Chair (6)](/images/G1830/Whale-Design-Chair-6_1.jpg)
### A Chair for 9 Common Postures in Discussion
In brainstorming sessions and other meetings that require active involvement, chairs allow free movement to facilitate in-depth discussion. Whale is the right seat for every task in a conference, enabling you to comfortably change to 9 common sitting postures from leaning toward speakers to turning around for a chat.
