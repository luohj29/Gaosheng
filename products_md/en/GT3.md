## Specification:
1. PASS BIFMA Certificate:
2. Backrest:good quality PP material with metal tube Φ25mm*T1.5mm. Can add backrest cushion. and the back no screw is the patented technology.
3. Seat cushion: 55 density molded foam with 12mm plywood covered by upholstery fabric.
4. Armrest:armrest with PP pad, height adjustable,it can be remove.
5. Mechanism: a self-weighing mechanism system with lifting and locking position and greater comfor for the users back, tilting(range from 90-108 degree)
6. Gaslift:Withus gaslift 100-40#.its service life is 120000 times up&down,
7. Base:R310mm nylon base pass BIFMA certificate. and it can under 1136 kgs pressure.
8. Caster: Dia 50mm nylon caster, BIFMA approval.
