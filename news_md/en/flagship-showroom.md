We are thrilled to announce the opening of our new flagship showroom in Shanghai. This 1,200 square meter space is designed not just as a display area, but as a collaborative laboratory.

## A Space for Co-Creation

Developers and designers can now work directly with our team to prototype show flats in real-time. The showroom features a vast material library, movable wall systems, and our complete collection of modular furniture.

![Showroom Interior](/images/home_second.png)

### Key Features

*   **Material Library**: Access to over 500 fabrics and finishes.
*   **Mock-up Zones**: Three distinct areas for living, dining, and workspace setups.
*   **Lighting Lab**: Test different lighting conditions to see how they affect textures and colors.

We invite you to book a private tour and experience the future of furniture staging.
