## Gaosheng Furniture Co., Ltd. provides ergonomic office chairs to schools in Saudi Arabia

[Foshan Gaosheng Furniture Co., Ltd.](https://www.gaoshenghk.com/), a leading modern office furniture solutions company, recently announced its successful cooperation with schools in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The company provides a range of ergonomically designed training chairs and office chairs to improve the learning and working environment for students and employees.

[Hong Kong Goldman International Co., Ltd.](https://www.gaoshenghk.com/about-us/), the parent company of Foshan Goldman Furniture Co., Ltd., is an enterprise integrating R&D, production and sales, and is committed to providing high-quality office furniture solutions. With office chairs and desks as its core products, the company has earned a reputation for innovative design and superior craftsmanship.

The recently supplied ergonomic office chairs and training chairs are part of Gaosheng’s extensive product line. Designed to provide comfort, support and functionality, these chairs meet the specific needs of educational institutions and office environments. [Features of the chair](https://www.gaoshenghk.com/luxury-modern-deluxe-design-with-wheel-leather-home-office-chair-gs-g1900-product/) include:

1.  Armrest: Made of aluminum alloy, with two options: polished and chrome plated.
2.  Mechanism: Multifunctional mechanism that allows tilting and locking, as well as height adjustment for personalized comfort.
3.  Gaslift: Originated from South Korea, providing adjustable height from 475mm to 550mm.
4.  Five-star base: Made of aluminum alloy with optional polished and chrome finishes.
5.  Casters: Equipped with 60mm large PU casters for smooth movement.

The ergonomic design of these chairs is designed to promote better posture, reduce stress on the body, and enhance overall health, especially when sitting for long periods of time. By using high-quality materials and advanced features, Gaosheng Furniture Co., Ltd. ensures that the chairs meet the highest standards of durability and performance.

The partnership with schools in Saudi Arabia demonstrates Gaosheng’s commitment to providing tailor-made solutions to educational institutions. Recognizing the importance of creating a conducive learning environment, the company works closely with partners in Saudi Arabia to understand their specific requirements and provide customized furniture solutions.
